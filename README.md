# PESTEL-Analysis-and-Revenue-Forecasting
PESTEL analysis of BMW Group focusing on supply chain and sales dynamics using real sales data. Includes political, economic, social, technological, environmental, and legal insights, plus ARIMA time-series forecasting to predict monthly revenue and support strategic decision-making.


## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **PESTEL analysis of the BMW Group** with a strong focus on **supply chain management and sales performance**. The analysis integrates macro-environmental factors with real BMW sales data to generate actionable strategic insights.

In addition, **time-series forecasting using ARIMA** is applied to predict BMWâ€™s future monthly revenue, supporting data-driven planning and decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze external macro-environmental factors affecting BMW using the **PESTEL framework**
- Understand how political, economic, social, technological, environmental, and legal forces influence supply chain and sales
- Perform **advanced analytics** using ARIMA to forecast revenue trends
- Provide strategic recommendations based on analytical findings

---

## Dataset Description

**BMW Sales Dataset** includes:
- Region
- BMW Model
- Year & Date
- Sales Volume
- Revenue
- Profit
- Cost
- Market Segment

The data was cleaned, formatted, and prepared using Python for reliable analysis.

---

## Methodology

### 1. Data Preparation
- Removed missing values
- Converted and validated date formats
- Ensured consistency across sales and financial variables

### 2. PESTEL Analysis
- **Political:** Trade policies, stability, EV incentives  
- **Economic:** Market pricing, purchasing power  
- **Social:** EV adoption, sustainability trends  
- **Technological:** Innovation, autonomous driving, AI  
- **Environmental:** Emission concerns, green mobility  
- **Legal:** Regulatory complexity across regions  

### 3. Advanced Analytics
- Seasonal decomposition of revenue
- **ARIMA forecasting** to predict monthly revenue for the next 12 months

---

## Key Insights

- Politically stable regions generate higher revenue
- Premium pricing aligns with stronger economies
- EV demand is rising steadily across markets
- Technology-driven models generate the highest revenue
- ARIMA forecasts indicate **steady revenue growth with seasonal variation**
